The Home Page is where you find out what’s happening and what the theme is for each day. Activities are grouped by day and clicking on each theme lets you dive right in!

What to do once you’re logged in

Watch the daily video:

Learn more about the conversation topics through interactive video resources. View the videos and gain points for the day.

Complete your activities for the day:

Answer a question, upload a photo or simply let us know what you think! Complete the daily challenge to earn extra points. But remember to be honest!

Ask Sim & his SGMC anything:

Always wondered why things are done a certain way? Have an idea that will be the next game changer? Or would you love some career advice from Sim? Send all of them in! They want to hear about what drives you and what you’d like to achieve in DBS. Ask and they will try to answer!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I win the daily prizes?

A: Those of you who that earn the most points each day will be selected daily to win a bonus prize. What’s more, if you watch the daily video you will be given the change to scratch and win! There are Foodpanda vouchers to be won each day! So what are you waiting for? Start earning those points! 6 prizes to be won daily and 3 grand prizes at the end of the week!

Q: How will I be notified that I’ve won a prize?

A: If you’ve been paying attention and have managed to earn the most points for the day, your name will appear as a daily winner on the leader board. The scratch and win game is an instant win so you will know immediately after playing what, if any, prize you have won. If you’re lucky enough to be a winner, the voucher you win will appear in your voucher wallet – simply visit ‘My Page’ to see your vouchers!

Q: How can I find out how many points I’ve earned so far?

A: You can visit ‘My Page’ at any time to see how many points you have accumulated. Remember, the more activities you complete, the more points you earn!
